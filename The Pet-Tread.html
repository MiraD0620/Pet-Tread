The Pet-Tread

The Pet-Tread is a dog-sized treadmill designed to help keep dog healthy and feel like they are walking in the park. We want to give dogs that may not have a chance to walk in the park often that experience. The Pet-Tread has a speaker to enhance the sound of a park, a turf ground for the feel, and a lightly smelling perfume that smells like a park for the owner to spray on. Pet-Tread is designed to help dogs and owners alike. We sell Pet-Tread for $399 with the option to buy extra bottles of smell spray. It takes very little setup and all you have to do is put in the time you want for your dog to run.

The perks of Pet-Tread...

  • A turf ground on the treadmill.
  • A speaker playing sounds you would hear at a park.
  • A scent spray with the scent of a park.
  • You do not need to do any work.
  • It is returnable up to 20 days after puchase.

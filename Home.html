Pet-Tread

Our mission is to create an immersive exercise experience for your pet, enhancing their well being and saving your time.

Bringing the best to you and your pet.

Our Mission...

Here at Pet-Tread we want to create the experience of a walk in the park for a dog who may not live near a park or is cooped up in a city. Our goal is to really create the experience of the sounds,feel,and smell of a park for a dog who may not often get that. We want to benefit all dogs and owners through Pet-Tread. Your dog's well being is our first priority along with your time.

Our Goals...

  • Create a better exercise experience for your pet.
  • Let dogs that may not have the chance to get daily walks get enough exercise.
  • Allow dogs who mostly walk on the pavement or in a city get the experience almost like walking in the park
  • Save your time.

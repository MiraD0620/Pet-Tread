Our Story

Our team consists of 5 founders who all came together to create Pet-Tread. Each person contributes a special aspect to our team. We came together in 2019 and had the idea for Pet-Tread after reading a book about a city dog stuck inside. When Covid hit, we realized that many dog owners did not want to go outside even to walk their dog! We decided to create the Pet-Tread which started as a normal dog treadmill and from there, we made it into a whole immersive experience for the dog so that it felt like a walk in the park.

A timeline of our story...

  1. In 2019, we came together for a book club and decided to create a business that benefited dogs.
  2. In 2020, the pandemic hit and we got the idea to create a dog treadmill.
  3. Three months later, in late March of 2020, we made the treadmill feel like a walk in the park.
  4. In 2021, we got the idea to come out with new models each year.
  5. Now we have a steady business going and are selling lots.
